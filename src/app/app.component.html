Body Profile

The first phase, Body Profile, is the most important segment of the My Fitbody Program. It's the building block that sets the foundation for the formulation of your ideal fitness plan.

In this phase the program gathers key factors that varies amongst individuals to eliminate guess work and to increase its accuracy.

Variables such as age, sex, weight, height, body type and a few others are all considered in how your set fitness goal can be efficiently attained.

The Body Profile phase plays the ultimate role in the integration process of the My Fitbody Program. Complete the segment below and get one step closer to your ideal fitness plan.
